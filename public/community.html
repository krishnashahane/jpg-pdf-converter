Community & Roadmap

ipdfjpg.com grows through user feedback. We publish what we are building, invite you to test new ideas, and highlight tips shared by the community.

Feedback that ships

Batch uploads, mobile-friendly drag-and-drop, and the “no data stored” badge all came from user requests. We review submissions weekly and prioritize changes that save time for classrooms, agencies, and independent creators.

Upcoming roadmap

On deck: PDF compression controls, multi-page PDF-to-image export, and additional image formats. We will share progress on the Blog and invite testers as features reach beta.

Beta testing

Want early access? Visit our Contact page. Beta testers get direct support and influence final tuning before public release.

Community guidelines

Be respectful, keep feedback focused on improving tools, and avoid sharing sensitive documents. Clear, actionable notes help us ship fixes quickly.

User tips we love

Rename files with numbers to control order, brighten scanned notes before uploading, and always preview converted files on mobile before sharing. Simple habits reduce friction for everyone.

Share your story

Tell us how you use the toolkit—assignments, proposals, print proofs, or creative portfolios. We highlight real workflows (with permission) to inspire other users.
