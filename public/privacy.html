Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 January 2025

Thank you for using ipdfjpg.com. We built this toolkit to be private, simple, and transparent. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By accessing ipdfjpg.com you agree to this notice and our Terms & Conditions.

1. Information we collect

Usage data. We receive basic technical data such as IP address, browser type/version, device type, operating system, pages visited, time on site, and referring URLs. This is aggregated to improve performance and detect abuse.

Files you upload. To convert or process files you may upload PDFs or images. Files are processed automatically solely to complete the requested action. We do not use file contents for profiling or advertising.

Cookies and similar technologies. We use essential cookies to remember preferences and analytics cookies to understand performance. You can manage cookies in your browser or via the Cookie Settings link on our pages.

2. How we use information

We use collected information to operate ipdfjpg.com, process your conversions, secure the service, monitor uptime, improve features, and display ads that fund the free tools. Data is processed under legitimate interest or your consent where required.

3. Data retention and file deletion

Uploaded files are stored only as long as needed to complete the requested conversion plus a short buffer for reliability. Automatic cleanup removes both source files and outputs thereafter. We do not keep manual archives of your files.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared only with trusted providers that help us host or secure the service, when required by law, or to investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents.

6. Security

All traffic runs over HTTPS. Infrastructure is hardened with restricted access, logging, and monitoring. No method is 100% secure, so please avoid uploading highly sensitive documents if you are uncomfortable using online tools.

7. Your rights and choices

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal information, and to withdraw consent. To make a request, contact us at support@ipdfjpg.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

8. Children’s privacy

ipdfjpg.com is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided data, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International use

Our service may be accessed globally. By using it, you understand that data may be processed in the country where our servers or providers operate, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date at the top shows when changes take effect. Continued use means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email support@ipdfjpg.com or use the form on our Contact page.
