Cookie Policy

Last updated: 01 January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how ipdfjpg.com uses cookies and similar technologies. Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember your actions and preferences over time.

What cookies we use

Essential cookies. Required for basic site functions such as remembering your session preferences and enabling uploads.

Analytics cookies. Help us understand how the site performs (e.g., page loads, errors, device types) so we can improve stability and speed. Data is aggregated.

Non-essential cookies. We may use non-essential cookies for site improvements. You can opt out via our consent banner.

How to manage cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings—view, delete, block all, or block specific sites. Blocking certain cookies may affect site functionality. Use the “Cookie settings” link in the footer to reopen the consent banner and adjust preferences for non-essential cookies.

Third-party cookies

Third-party services we integrate with may set their own cookies. Their policies govern how they handle that data.

Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology, law, or our practices. The updated date indicates when changes take effect.
